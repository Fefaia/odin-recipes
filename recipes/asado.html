Asado

Description

The best way to cook meat

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Salt
  • Matches
  • Coal
  • Wood
  • Newspaper
  • Parrilla

Steps

  1. Make balls with the newspaper and gather them in a spot
  2. Put some coal over the newspaper balls
  3. Put more coal over the newly mountain or some wood branches
  4. Use the matches to start the fire, burn the papers
  5. Wait for the papers to burn completely and start burning the coal
  6. Wait till the coal start to become white
  7. In the meantime, start salting the meat
  8. Spread the white coal under the parrilla
  9. Wait a couple of minutes, till the parrilla start warming up
  10. Put the meat over the parrilla
  11. Wait till that side has been cooked and turn the meat to the other side
  12. Wait till the otherside is cooked
  13. Serve
